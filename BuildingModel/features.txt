The features selected for the prediction are the number of rooms, bathrooms and the year the house was built.
These features are chosen based on the analysis of the correlation matrix. The highest correlation of price is with 
the distace(-0.21), the number of rooms(0.39), bedrooms(0.3), bathrooms(0.32), cars(0.14) and the year the house was 
built(-0.21). But the number of bedrooms is rejected as a feature due to a high correlation with the number of 
rooms(0.77). The correlation of the remainining features is between 0.1 to -0.1. Hence, these are also rejected.
